Our Products

Explore our comprehensive range of high-performance CNC machine tools, designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern manufacturing.

Wire EDM Machines

High-speed, high-precision cutting for complex shapes and molds.

New Spark W400

A high-performance wire EDM machine designed for precision mold and die making. Features advanced auto-threading and a stable thermal displacement compensation system.

New Spark W600

A larger capacity model for bigger workpieces without compromising on precision. Ideal for automotive and large-scale tool manufacturing.

Sinker EDM Machines

Ideal for die and mold making, creating intricate cavities and shapes.

New Spark S50

Versatile sinker EDM for high-precision mold and component manufacturing. Delivers excellent surface finishes and complex cavity machining.

Hole Drilling EDM

Fast and precise drilling of small, deep holes in conductive materials.

New Spark D703

High-speed EDM drilling machine for creating starter holes, cooling channels, and micro-holes with exceptional speed and accuracy.

Smart Manufacturing Lines

Integrated automation solutions for the factory of the future.

New Spark Automation Cell

A fully integrated manufacturing cell combining multiple EDM machines with robotic loading/unloading, CMM inspection, and central management software for unattended "lights-out" production.

Industry Applications

Our machines provide critical solutions across a wide range of demanding industries, enabling innovation and excellence.

Aerospace

Manufacturing turbine blades, engine components, and structural parts with extreme precision and reliability from superalloys.

Automotive

Producing injection molds for dashboards, engine blocks, and transmission components with high efficiency and accuracy.

Medical Devices

Creating surgical instruments, implants, and orthopedic devices from titanium and stainless steel with biocompatible finishes.

Electronics

Fabricating micro-connectors, semiconductor molds, and mobile device casings with microscopic precision.

Tool & Die

Crafting high-precision stamping dies, extrusion dies, and complex molds for mass production with exceptional longevity.

Energy Sector

Manufacturing components for power generation turbines, drilling equipment, and renewable energy systems.

Our Story

Founded on the principles of innovation, precision, and customer satisfaction, Suzhou New Spark Machine Tool Co., Ltd. has grown into a globally recognized manufacturer of advanced CNC solutions. From our state-of-the-art facility in Suzhou, we dedicate ourselves to research, development, and production of machine tools that redefine industry standards.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians works tirelessly to integrate the latest technologies, ensuring that every machine we deliver offers unparalleled performance, reliability, and value to our clients worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower manufacturers with innovative and reliable machine tools that enhance productivity, precision, and profitability.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in the machine tool industry, renowned for our technological advancements and customer-centric approach.

Our Values

Quality, Innovation, Integrity, and Partnership. These principles guide every decision we make and every machine we build.
